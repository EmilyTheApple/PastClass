Contrast

The design principle of contrast is used on this webpage, a site for knitting patterns called Knitty. The navigation is on the left hand side of the screen and has a dark grey background instead of the white background that the rest of the page has. The type in the nav is also a light color, which helps us to be able to read it clearly. This contrast helps us to navigate the site more easily since we can tell what elements on the page are part of the page and what elements are part of the navigation. This website can be found here.
