Repetition

The design principle of repetition can be seen in the example here, a page from Rick Riordan's website. The text colors in the header and nav are all the same and the image used for the bullet point is repeated in the nav and also in the main below the image. It helps to tie the website's design together and continues as a theme throughout the whole website. This webpage can be found here.
