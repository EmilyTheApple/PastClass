Typography

The design principle of typography, or rather of good typography practice, is shown here, on the Ultimate Guitar website. Each section of the page has chosen a different font. At the top, the title of the song and artist appear in a simple to read sans-serif font. The next section, "Chord Diagrams" is a different font, bold, and all caps so that it is easy to pick out of the page. Under the advertisement for the app and below are the lyrics of the song and the accompanying chords. These appear in a monotype font, which isn't commonly seen on the web and lets us know that that content is not part of any of the other sections. This webpage can be found here.
