Alignment

The design principle of alignment is shown here, on the W3C Developers website. Though the image at the top of the screen is aligned to the left of the screen and the text is center aligned, the beginning of the text and the bottom left corner of the image are aligned letting us know that the two elements are related, though not part of the same flow. The explore button is also aligned to the middle like the text above it, showing us that there's a natural progression between reading the text and clicking the button.
