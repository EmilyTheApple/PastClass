Missionary Suites

Our delux missionary suites come in two options: Queen Missionary Suite and King Missionary Suite. The only difference between the two is the size of the single bed; queen sized and king sized.

Other features of the room are a bathroom, of course, sitting/living room with a connected kitchenette, and a separate bedroom. There are two desks in each room.
